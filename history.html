Our Story

Our Heritage

St. Mary's Church has served Mandalay's Anglican community for generations. Rooted in the Christian tradition and shaped by the life of the city, our church has been a place of worship, fellowship, and service.

Through seasons of joy and challenge, our congregation has remained committed to gathering in prayer, supporting one another, and reaching out to our neighbors with Christ's love.

Anglican Tradition

As part of the worldwide Anglican Communion, we value Scripture, tradition, and reason, and gather around Word and sacrament. Our worship draws from the rich heritage of Anglican liturgy while remaining accessible and meaningful to all who join us.

We seek to be a church that honors both ancient wisdom and contemporary needs, finding in our Anglican identity a way to be both rooted and relevant.

Community Life

Members, families, and friends share in prayer, music, learning, and acts of service. Our church family includes people from many walks of life, united in our desire to grow in faith and love.

Whether through Sunday worship, midweek prayer, community meals, or service projects, we strive to create meaningful connections and opportunities for spiritual growth.
